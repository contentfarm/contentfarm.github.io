Typeracer

Typeracer is a really fun way to increase your typing spead. You get to type text from stories, songs, and more. One thing we really like about Typeracer compared to Nitrotype is that you have to use the backspace button if you make a mistake. This may sound trivial, but many typing services allow you to keep typing without correcting your mistakes. Best of all, you can start typing without an account!
